# Math Genius — Locations

Descriptions of the different areas where the stories take place.

---

## The Neighborhood

The neighborhood is where most of the characters live. It's a cute little neighborhood where everyone knows and plays with each other.

### Layout

Humphrey and Hailey live with their mom at the edge of the neighborhood on top of a hill. From their house, a road with a sidewalk stretches past the homes of their neighbors in order:

1. **Roo's house** — Roo lives here with her mom, Kanga.
2. **Lulu's house** — Lulu the Kangaroo lives next door to Roo.
3. **Margret and Maggie's house** — The tiger sisters live here together.
4. **Tigey and Tigey Avinia's house** — The other tiger sisters.

Wooden fences run between the houses, and the neighbors peek over them to say hello when Humphrey walks down the street.

At the end of the street is **Giri's chocolate shop**, a beloved destination on Humphrey's morning walk.

### Kiki's Lasting Changes to the Neighborhood

Kiki's experiments have permanently altered the neighborhood in several memorable ways:

1. **The Ice Cream Sledding Hill** — The tall hill at the playground has been covered in ice cream. Kids use it for sledding 🛷. It's usually chocolate flavor, but Kiki needs to restock it every few days, so the flavor changes sometimes. The kids also eat it — because, well, it's ice cream! 🍨

2. **The Cheese Tree Forest** — Kiki planted a forest on one side of town after an earlier experiment knocked down all the trees. However, the trees she planted were genetically modified "Cheese Trees." The trunk is like a cheese stick and the branches are also made of cheese 🧀. When branches fall in the wind, the townsfolk pick them up to snack on. Kiki harvests and sells the cheese at the supermarket, using the proceeds to fund her other mad scientist experiments 🌲.

3. **Kiki's House / Laboratory** — Kiki has had to rebuild her house and lab multiple times due to experiments gone wrong. The current version has **10 basement levels** (they survive even if the top floor explodes). The main floor has an elevator that goes very fast to the bottom, and an escalator that also goes down. Below the 10th basement floor lies **The Undergrowth**, where cave-dwelling friends and fairies live. The main floor also houses a sandwich shop run by **Lilli**, Giri's niece — a kid Giraffe.

4. **The Hot Chocolate Spring** — Kiki transformed the neighborhood pond into a hot chocolate spring. People love stopping by in the morning to scoop up their hot chocolate ☕.

5. **Kiki's Iron Man Suit** — Kiki always wears a utility belt. At the press of a button, an iron man suit deploys around her. It's usually for protection when an experiment goes wrong or a villain shows up. The suit can fly and shoot lasers, and Kiki can fly roughly as fast as Humphrey can run — which, because he's a heffalump, is very fast.

6. **Kiki's Spaceship Fleet** — Kiki built her first spaceship during the saga where the kids confronted Bermione on Jupiter when Bermione was the villain ruler there. That saga ended with Bermione reforming and moving in with her sister Hermione. Today, Kiki funds her fleet through her cheese business, being the landlord for Lilli's sandwich shop, and having the only commercial access to and from the Undergrowth.

7. **The Donut River** — Kiki accidentally created a fast-flowing donut river that stretches from the town all the way to the volcano 🍩. Each donut is large enough for a kid to ride on. The river is dangerous and can pull the kids along farther than they mean to go. The donut river flows all the way to the base of the volcano, where the giant volcano dwellers live (their leader is called Giganta). The giants eat the donuts as part of their well-balanced diet of rocks.

### Other Notable Features

- **Margret's Backyard Pool** — Margret's house has a pool in the backyard, surrounded by the wooden fence. The neighborhood's mermaid friends use their water teleportation power to visit through this pool.

- **The Lake** — A large lake near the neighborhood. Humphrey sometimes runs through it along the bottom, keeping his trunk above the water to breathe. The kids sometimes build very large, elaborate sandcastle forts the size of actual houses at the lake.

- **The Pine Tree Forest (East Side)** — On the opposite side of town from the Cheese Tree Forest is a normal pine tree forest — except some of the trees are alive. They are led by **Mrs. Spruce** and help guard the town on the east side from villains attempting to take over. They are not always very effective, as villains often fly over or slip through in sneaky ways.

- **Peggy the Pegasus** — Peggy serves as a sort of police officer for the neighborhood. She makes sure everyone stays safe and that the rules are followed.

- **The Volcano (visible from just outside town)** — You cannot see the volcano from within the town itself, but if you walk just outside the neighborhood, the peak becomes visible in the distance. A big grassy prairie stretches between the town and the volcano, with the donut river flowing through it all the way to the volcano's base.
